# All-Time Favorite Movies

## Top 20

### 1. Inception (2010)
**Director**: Christopher Nolan
**Genre**: Sci-Fi/Thriller
**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Why**: Mind-bending, layered storytelling. Rewatched 5+ times.
**Memorable**: "You're waiting for a train..."

### 2. The Shawshank Redemption (1994)
**Director**: Frank Darabont
**Genre**: Drama
**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Why**: Hope perseverance, perfect pacing
**Quote**: "Get busy living or get busy dying"

### 3. The Matrix (1999)
**Directors**: Wachowskis
**Genre**: Sci-Fi/Action
**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Why**: Revolutionary. Changed action films forever.
**Impact**: Made me question reality at age 12

### 4. Interstellar (2014)
**Director**: Christopher Nolan
**Genre**: Sci-Fi/Drama
**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Why**: Emotional + science. Cried in theater.
**Soundtrack**: Hans Zimmer's best work

### 5. Parasite (2019)
**Director**: Bong Joon-ho
**Genre**: Thriller/Drama
**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Why**: Perfect social commentary, genre-bending
**Note**: Watched 3 times in theaters

### 6. The Social Network (2010)
**Director**: David Fincher
**Genre**: Drama
**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Why**: As a developer, this hits different
**Soundtrack**: Trent Reznor/Atticus Ross perfection

### 7. Mad Max: Fury Road (2015)
**Director**: George Miller
**Genre**: Action
**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Why**: Best pure action film ever made
**Fact**: Saw it in theaters 4 times

### 8. The Grand Budapest Hotel (2014)
**Director**: Wes Anderson
**Genre**: Comedy/Drama
**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Why**: Visual perfection, charming story
**Style**: Peak Wes Anderson

### 9. Arrival (2016)
**Director**: Denis Villeneuve
**Genre**: Sci-Fi
**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Why**: Linguistics + aliens. Emotional gut-punch.
**Based on**: Ted Chiang's "Story of Your Life"

### 10. Everything Everywhere All at Once (2022)
**Directors**: Daniels
**Genre**: Sci-Fi/Comedy
**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Why**: Chaotic, heartfelt, original
**Hot take**: Better than Multiverse of Madness
