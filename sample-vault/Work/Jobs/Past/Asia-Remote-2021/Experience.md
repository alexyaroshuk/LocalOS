# Digital Nomad Experience - Asia 2021

## Overview
6-month remote work stint across Asia while working for StartupXYZ
- **Role**: Full-stack Engineer
- **Arrangement**: Fully remote, async-first company
- **Countries**: Thailand, Vietnam, Japan

## The Journey

### Thailand (March - April 2021)
**Bangkok** - 4 weeks
- Stayed in: Sukhumvit area
- Coworking: HUBBA Thailand
- Internet: Excellent (100+ Mbps)
- Cost: ~$1500/month all-in
- Highlights:
  - Amazing street food
  - Great coffee shops for work
  - Met other digital nomads at coworking space
  - See: [[Travel/Asia/Thailand/Bangkok Food Guide|Bangkok Food Guide]]

**Chiang Mai** - 2 weeks
- Digital nomad hub!
- Super affordable
- Slower pace than Bangkok
- Great for focused deep work

### Vietnam (May - June 2021)
**Hanoi** - 3 weeks
- Stayed in: Old Quarter
- Coworking: Toong Coworking
- Coffee culture is next level
- Motorbike chaos is real
- See: [[Travel/Asia/Vietnam/Ha Long Bay Trip|Ha Long Bay Trip]]

**Ho Chi Minh City** - 3 weeks
- More modern, faster pace
- Better infrastructure
- Rooftop bars after work 🌃

### Japan (July - August 2021)
**Tokyo** - 6 weeks
- Most expensive stop but worth it
- Stayed in: Shibuya
- Coworking: WeWork
- Internet: Blazing fast
- Vending machine coffee at 3am was a lifesaver
- See: [[Travel/Asia/Japan/Tokyo 2023|Tokyo Notes]]

## Work Experience

### Timezone Management
Working PST hours from Asia
- Bangkok: 2pm - 10pm (perfect for me)
- Tokyo: 1am - 9am (rough but doable)
- Lots of async communication
- Recorded video updates for standup

### Productivity
- **Best**: Early morning work sessions before the day started
- **Worst**: Fighting timezone fatigue in Japan
- **Solution**: Adjusted to local time, worked odd hours
- Actually got MORE done without office distractions

### Challenges
1. **Connectivity**: Had backups (portable WiFi, mobile hotspot)
2. **Time zones**: Overlap with team for 2-4 hours daily
3. **Work-life balance**: Too easy to explore vs. work
4. **Loneliness**: Missed the team occasionally

## Finances
- **Total cost**: ~$12,000 for 6 months
- **Rent saved back home**: ~$8,000
- **Net cost**: $4,000 for 6 months in Asia
- Incredible value!

## Tools That Made It Possible
- Notion for async documentation
- Zoom for daily standups
- Slack for quick comms
- Google Calendar (lived in multiple timezones)
- Portable WiFi router
- Good headphones (noise cancelling essential)

## Would I Do It Again?
**Absolutely!** But next time:
- Stay 2-3 months per location (too rushed)
- Better planning of coworking spaces
- Set stricter work hours (too easy to overwork)
- Maybe do it with a remote-first company from the start

## Impact on Career
- Learned async communication (crucial skill)
- Became more independent and self-directed
- Built global network of other nomads
- Proved remote work is viable for engineering
- Led to current remote flexibility at TechCorp

This experience shaped my preference for remote-friendly companies. The freedom was life-changing.
